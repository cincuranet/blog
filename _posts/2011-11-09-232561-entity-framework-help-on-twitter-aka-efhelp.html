--- title: "Entity Framework help on Twitter aka #efhelp" date: 2011-11-09T16:12:12Z tags: - Entity Framework category: none layout: post ---

If you're looking for a help related to Entity Framework and you're on Twitter (mine is @cincura_net) you can try asking with hashtag #efhelp. Sure, no promises about getting reply, but this hashtag is monitored with a lot of skilled people. If it not fits into 140 characters, you might also find somebody willing to help you using different channel (i.e. email, StackOverflow, on-site, ...).
